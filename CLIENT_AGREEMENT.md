## 4. REVISION POLICY

### 4.1 Included Revisions

This Agreement includes **two (2) rounds of revisions** during the design and development process. A "revision round" is defined as a single consolidated set of written change requests submitted by the Client.

### 4.2 What Constitutes a Revision

Revisions include reasonable modifications to:
- Layout and page structure
- Color adjustments within the established design direction
- Typography changes
- Content placement and ordering
- Image swaps or repositioning
- Minor functional adjustments within the original scope

### 4.3 What Does NOT Constitute a Revision

The following are considered scope changes, not revisions, and may require a separate quote:
- Fundamental redesign or change of creative direction after mockup approval
- Addition of new pages or sections not included in the original scope
- Addition of new features or functionality
- Changes driven by new business requirements introduced after project start
- Third-party integration work not included in the original scope

### 4.4 Additional Revisions

Revision requests beyond the two included rounds will be billed at **$75 per additional round**, or as otherwise agreed in writing. Client will be notified and must approve any additional revision charges before work proceeds.
