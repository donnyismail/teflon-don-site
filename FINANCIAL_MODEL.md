# 3. PRICING OPTIMIZATION

## 3A. Should $499 Be Higher?

### Houston Market Comparison

| Provider Type | Basic Website | Mid-Tier | Full Service |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Teflon Don (current)** | **$499** | **$999** | **$2,499** |
| Houston web agencies | $3,000-8,000 | $8,000-15,000 | $15,000-30,000 |
| National freelancers (Upwork) | $500-2,000 | $2,000-5,000 | $5,000-12,000 |
| Wix/Squarespace DIY | $200-400/yr | N/A | N/A |
| Fiverr bottom-tier | $100-300 | $300-800 | $1,000-3,000 |
| Local solo developers (TX) | $800-2,500 | $2,500-5,000 | $5,000-10,000 |

### Analysis

**$499 is underpriced for the Houston market.**

The $499 price point is effective as a door-opener -- it eliminates
price objection and gets you in the door with small business owners
who have been quoted $5K+ by agencies. However, the effective hourly
rate of $36.43 is below sustainable. Here are two approaches:

#### Option A: Keep $499 as Loss Leader (Recommended Short-Term)

Keep $499 as the hook. The real money comes from:
- Upselling to Tier 2/3 during the audit call
- Selling maintenance retainers after delivery
- Referral volume (low price = more clients = more word of mouth)

**Expected outcome:** 60% of Tier 1 inquiries upsell to Tier 2 or 3.
Blended average deal rises from $1,099 to $1,350+.

#### Option B: Raise Tier 1 to $799 (Recommended at Month 6+)

Once you have 15+ portfolio pieces and testimonials:
- Tier 1: $799 (effective hourly: $62.52 -- sustainable)
- Tier 2: $1,499 (effective hourly: $53.10 -- better)
- Tier 3: $3,499 (effective hourly: $66.76 -- strong)

**Projected impact:** 15-20% fewer leads but 60% higher revenue per deal.
Net revenue increase of ~28%.

#### Recommended Pricing Timeline

| Period | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Tier 3 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Months 1-6 (current) | $499 | $999 | $2,499 |
| Months 7-12 | $699 | $1,299 | $2,999 |

### Recommended Structure

- Eliminates collections risk entirely
- Small business owners are accustomed to paying small amounts upfront

- The free mockup already de-risks the purchase for the client
